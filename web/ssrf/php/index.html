The Whiterose Foundation was started by Minister Zhi Zhang 'Whiterose' to not only help fund the struggle against the social and economic issues all over the world, but to also raise awareness among the masses about these issues and gather unity to make this world a better place.

Minister Zhi Zhang 'Whiterose'

Zhi Zhang aka Whiterose is the Chinese Minister of State Security and founder of the world's biggest commonwealth, the Deus Group. A philanthropist by heart, she has donated over 100 Million USD worth to charity to bring about a better change in the world. Whiterose founded The Whiterose foundation as a step towards the change.
